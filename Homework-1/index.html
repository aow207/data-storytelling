Malaria Outbreaks Buzz Around Washington

With the rise of the anti-vaccination movement, preventable diseases, like measles, have been flaring up throughout the United States. However, there is currently no licensed malaria vaccine on the market. As of July 2019, Washington has seen the most cases of Malaria with 40 incidents this year.
